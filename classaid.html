ClassAiD - Inter class collaboration tool

ClassAiD is an advanced class management tool that lets you bring your entire class within a single network. Class representatives can send notice and set class schedules. Everyone in a single network can chat in a room.

Simple Login Process

Login or signup within a few steps. No email is required. Just enter your username and password, and you are in. You will have to give a little more information about yourself if you log in for the first time. Consider it as the signup process. We do not store your username or password. So, do not bother to forget it unless you want to lose your account.

Use as a Student

If you want to get notify about your class schedule, get the latest update about your class, and all your collegiate and teachersâ€™ information all in one place, we got your covered. Sign up to ClassAiD and we will send a request to your class admin. Once the request is approved, all is set. You will get your class schedule and notices in real-time. You can gossip with your classmates too using our built-in chat engine. Just do not forget to enter the accurate team code and always remember your login credentials. It is that simple.

Use with privileges

If you are a class admin or representative, you are the sun here. Send notices, set class schedules, accept, or reject connection requests, and organize your class. Do not forget to share your team code otherwise, no one will be able to join your list.
